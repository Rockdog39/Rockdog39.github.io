Communication

Communication is something that I am finding myself constantly improving, and in recent years have learnt that it's not just been able to get a message across such as in my youTube exmple but also being able to listen and understand others. I have found that in the workplace, communication is key to getting things done and working well with others. I have had to learn how to communicate effectively with different types of people, and how to adapt my communication style to different situations. I have also had to learn how to communicate complex ideas in a way that is easy for others to understand. I have found that good communication skills are essential for building relationships, resolving conflicts, and achieving goals in the workplace.

Emotional Intelligence

Through my studies in Communication subjects and my ability to experiment with conecpts learnt in real workplace situations, I have developed a strong understanding of the importance of Emotional Intelligence in the workplace.
I have found that being able to understand and manage my own emotions, as well as the emotions of others, is crucial for effective communication and building strong relationships in the workplace. I have also learnt that emotional intelligence can help to resolve conflicts, improve teamwork, and increase overall job satisfaction.

Self Discovery

The realisation that I can improve my ability to communicate effectively with others has been a huge step in my personal and professional development. Through concepts such as the Johari Window, I have been able to gain a better understanding of myself and how I communicate with others. This has assisted me in being better able to express myself and understand others, which has improved my relationships and communication in the workplace. These skills, albeit ever evolving will be an important part of my personal and professional growth as I continue to learn and develop in my career.

Leadership

Through great communication, I have been able to develop strong leadership skills. I have found that being able to communicate effectively with others is essential for leading a team and achieving goals. I have also found that good communication skills are essential for building trust and respect with team members, which is crucial for effective leadership. I have been able to use my communication skills to inspire and motivate others, and to create a positive and productive work environment.

Overall, I believe that my communication skills have been a key factor in my ability to develop strong leadership skills and to be an effective leader in the workplace.
